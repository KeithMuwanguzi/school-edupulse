What goes wrong with school systems today

Most tools in Uganda are bloated, expensive, or built elsewhere and adapted late.

Term data gets mixed

Grades from Term 1 appear in Term 2. SkulPulse isolates every year and term completely.

You pay for unused modules

Subscribe to students, finance, AI, or all of them. Remove what you don't need next term.

Parents are last to know

Report cards and fee balances online. MTN MoMo and Airtel Money built in.

Problems found too late

AI tracks attendance and marks over time, not just end-of-term averages.
